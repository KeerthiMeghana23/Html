A Few Words About the Institute

Welcome to Sri Vasavi Institute of Engineering and Technology established
with a vision to create engineering professionals of exceptional caliber,
SVIET College is a prestigious institution located in the vibrant city of
Machilipatnam, Andhra Pradesh. With a rich heritage spanning several decades.


At SVIET, we take immense pride in our commitment to providing a transformative learning experience.
Our dedicated faculty members, known for their expertise and passion, go above and beyond to inspire and
guide students towards achieving their full potential. Through a holistic approach to education, we empower
our students with not only technical knowledge but also the skills and values necessary to excel in a rapidly evolving world.
